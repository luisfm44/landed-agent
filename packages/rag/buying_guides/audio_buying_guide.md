# Audio Buying Guide for Landed

## Closed-back headphones

Closed-back headphones are useful for recording, tracking, monitoring, podcasting, and working in noisy environments. They provide isolation and reduce sound leakage.

For critical mixing, open-back headphones are often preferred because they usually provide a wider soundstage and a more natural sense of space. However, closed-back headphones can still be useful for production, editing, monitoring, and checking mixes in untreated rooms.

## Open-back headphones

Open-back headphones are often preferred for mixing and mastering because they can provide a more spacious and natural presentation.

Tradeoffs:
- They leak sound
- They do not isolate from noise
- They are not ideal for recording near microphones
- They work best in quiet environments

## Buying local vs importing in Colombia

When buying audio products in Colombia, users should compare:
- local price
- landed import cost
- shipping
- taxes
- warranty
- delivery time
- seller reliability
- return difficulty

Importing can be attractive when the landed cost is significantly lower than the local price. If the difference is small, buying locally is often safer because of warranty, faster delivery, and easier returns.

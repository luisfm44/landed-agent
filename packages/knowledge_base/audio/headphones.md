# Headphones Knowledge Base

## Sennheiser HD 800 S
Open-back audiophile headphone. Excellent soundstage, imaging and detail retrieval.
Recommended for classical music, orchestral music, analytical listening and competitive gaming because of its imaging.
Not ideal for noisy environments. Requires good amplification.

## Focal Clear
Open-back dynamic headphone. Strong dynamics, good punch and natural tone.
Good for rock, jazz and general audiophile listening.

## Beyerdynamic DT 1990 Pro
Open-back headphone with strong detail retrieval and sharp treble.
Good for mixing, mastering and gaming, but can be fatiguing for treble-sensitive users.

## Sony WH-1000XM5
Wireless ANC headphone. Good for travel, office work and noise cancellation.
Not considered true audiophile level compared with wired open-back headphones.

## HiFiMAN Edition XS
Planar magnetic open-back headphone. Wide soundstage, good detail and strong value.
Good for music, gaming and immersive listening, but requires decent amplification.
